\## Brand \& Style



This design system is crafted for an elite educational institution, blending academic rigor with a modern, forward-looking aesthetic. The brand personality is \*\*Trustworthy, Sophisticated, and Result-Oriented\*\*. It aims to evoke a sense of security and prestige for parents, while remaining accessible and motivating for students.



The design style is \*\*Corporate / Modern\*\* with a focus on \*\*Minimalism\*\*. It utilizes expansive white space to denote clarity and focus, paired with high-fidelity UI elements like soft shadows and precise typography. The aesthetic reflects a premium "VIP" educational experience, prioritizing information hierarchy and ease of navigation for both promotional content and administrative tasks.



\## Colors



The palette is anchored by \*\*Deep Navy (#1A3D5D)\*\*, representing stability, authority, and the institutional heritage of education. This is complemented by \*\*Professional Gold (#D4AF37)\*\*, which serves as a prestige accent for badges, high-level headers, and "VIP" designations.



\*\*Accent Orange (#F15A24)\*\* is used sparingly for critical functional alerts, such as exam registration buttons or urgent announcements. The neutral foundation relies on \*\*Clean White (#FFFFFF)\*\* and \*\*Surface Gray (#F7F7F7)\*\* to maintain a high-fidelity, airy feel, while \*\*Slate Text (#4A5568)\*\* ensures optimal readability without the harshness of pure black.



\## Typography



The design system exclusively uses \*\*Montserrat\*\* to achieve a modern, geometric, and clean look. Montserrat’s varied weights allow for a strong contrast between bold, authoritative headlines and highly legible body text.



\- \*\*Headlines:\*\* Use Bold (700) or SemiBold (600) weights. Display sizes should use slight negative letter spacing to feel more cohesive.

\- \*\*Body Text:\*\* Standardized at 16px for desktop to ensure comfort during long reading sessions (curriculum details, blog posts).

\- \*\*Labels:\*\* Always uppercase with increased letter spacing for navigation and overlines to provide a professional, organized structure.



\## Layout \& Spacing



The layout follows a \*\*12-column fixed grid\*\* for desktop, maxing out at 1200px to ensure readability is never compromised by extreme line lengths. A base 8px spacing scale governs all padding and margins to maintain rhythmic consistency.



\- \*\*Mobile:\*\* 4-column grid with 16px side margins. Content stacks vertically.

\- \*\*Tablet:\*\* 8-column grid with 24px margins.

\- \*\*Desktop:\*\* 12-column grid. Generous vertical section padding (80px to 120px) is used to separate educational programs and create a premium, "breathable" experience.

\- \*\*Dashboard View:\*\* For admin widgets, a fluid layout is permitted within the main content area, using a 24px gutter between cards.



\## Elevation \& Depth



Hierarchy is established using \*\*Tonal Layers\*\* and \*\*Ambient Shadows\*\*. This design system avoids heavy borders, preferring to use depth to define interactive surfaces.



\- \*\*Level 0 (Base):\*\* White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray (#F7F7F7).

\- \*\*Level 1 (Cards/Widgets):\*\* White background with a very soft, diffused shadow (0px 4px 20px, 5% opacity Navy).

\- \*\*Level 2 (Hover/Active):\*\* Slightly deeper shadow (0px 8px 30px, 10% opacity Navy) and a subtle 1px border in Gold for primary actions.

\- \*\*Overlays (Modals):\*\* High elevation with a 20% opacity backdrop blur to maintain focus on the registration or login forms.



\## Shapes



The design system uses a \*\*Rounded\*\* (0.5rem) shape language. This softens the professional tone, making the institution feel approachable and modern rather than overly rigid.



\- \*\*Buttons \& Inputs:\*\* 8px (0.5rem) corner radius.

\- \*\*Cards \& Dashboard Widgets:\*\* 16px (1rem) corner radius to emphasize their containing nature.

\- \*\*Chips/Badges:\*\* Pill-shaped (fully rounded) to contrast against structural elements.



\## Components



\### Buttons

\- \*\*Primary:\*\* Deep Navy background, White text. 8px radius. Subtle scale-up on hover.

\- \*\*Secondary:\*\* Gold background or Gold outline. Reserved for "VIP" or "Success" related actions.

\- \*\*Ghost:\*\* Navy outline or plain text with icon. Used for secondary navigation or "Read More".



\### Cards (Educational Programs)

\- White background, 16px radius, Level 1 shadow.

\- Feature a high-quality image header, Navy title (Headline-MD), and a Gold "Examine" button.



\### Dashboard Widgets

\- Modular 1x1 or 2x1 grid units. 

\- Use "Surface-Gray" headers to distinguish internal titles. 

\- Integrated progress bars use Gold to show student achievement.



\### Forms \& Inputs

\- Large, clear touch targets (48px height).

\- 1px Slate-Text border that thickens and turns Navy on focus.

\- Floating labels for a modern, space-efficient feel in admin panels.



\### Navigation

\- \*\*Desktop:\*\* Sticky transparent-to-white header. Navy links with a Gold underline effect on hover.

\- \*\*Admin Sidebar:\*\* Deep Navy background with collapsed/expanded states. Active state indicated by a Gold vertical bar on the left edge.
